  2. 点击"班次切换审批"

- **优先级**: P0

#### TC-014: 审批通过后班次和水牌变更
- **数据库验证**:
  ```bash
  sqlite3 /TG/tgservice/db/tgservice.db "SELECT shift FROM coaches WHERE phone='16675852676';"
  sqlite3 /TG/tgservice/db/tgservice.db "SELECT wb.status FROM water_boards wb JOIN coaches c ON wb.coach_no = c.coach_no WHERE c.phone='16675852676';"
  ```
- **验证**: shift 字段变更，水牌状态变为对应班次空闲
- **优先级**: P0

#### TC-015: 班次切换每月2次限制
